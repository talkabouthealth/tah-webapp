#{extends 'static.html' /} #{set pageHeader:'FAQ' /} 1. What is TalkAboutHealth?
TalkAboutHealth is a real-time support group where you can connect with others who have similar health concerns and interests for live, anonymous conversations.


2. How does TalkAboutHealth work?
You send a question or comment to TalkAboutHealth through the web site or by instant message. TalkAboutHealth immediately connects you with the right people for a live group or one on one conversation via audio, video, or instant message. In addition, TalkAboutHealth can notify you by instant message of conversations that might be of interest to you.


3. How long will it take to join a conversation?
Instantly.


4. What can I use TalkAboutHealth for?
You can meet others with similar interests and health concerns to expand your support network.
You can learn from peers and immediately benefit from the wisdom of other people who have been through similar experiences.
You can share your experiences, daily struggles, and triumphs to help others and increase the knowledge of the community.
You can talk to someone who understands for instant support.


 5. What else can I do on TalkAboutHealth?
You can browse or search for live conversations.
You can search and view archives of conversations based on key word and user names.


 6. Who can use TalkAboutHealth?
Patients, survivors, caregivers, family members, friends, physicians, pharmacists, nurses, therapists. Everyone in the health community is encouraged to share, request help, and provide support.


7. How do I get started?
Go to www.TalkAboutHealth.com and click "Sign-Up." Signing up only takes a minute.


8. What are "Thank you's"?
A "Thank you" is given by members to each other to show gratitude. If someone helps you, please give them a "Thank you."


 9. What levels of recognition can members achieve?
New Member - 0 to 5 conversations
Supporter - 5 to 15 conversations
Companion - 15 to 35 conversations
Advocate - 35 to 75 conversations
Fellow - 75 to 200 conversations
Benefactor - 200 to 500 conversations
Patron - 500 to 1,000 conversations
Champion - over 1,000 conversations


 10. Is TalkAboutHealth anonymous?
Yes. You will only be identified by your user name during conversations. You determine how much information to share in your profile and whether your profile is viewable or private to the community.


11. How do I protect my privacy?
By default, the profile information you provide is private. You may go to the "Profile Settings" page to make your profile information viewable to the community.
TalkAboutHealth does not share personally identifiable information such as your name and email address without your explicit permission.


 12. Are TalkAboutHealth conversations public?
All conversations on TalkAboutHealth can be found by internet search engines and accessed by the public.


13. Is it free?
Yes.


14. I forgot my password. What should I do?
To reset your password, go to Forgot Password and enter the email address you used to set up your account. An email will be sent to you with a new password.


15. How do I contact TalkAboutHealth support?
Send an e-mail to support@talkabouthealth.com. We are happy to help and always available to give assistance.


16. Why don't you have a community for my health issue?
We will in the future. Please let us know your request at support@talkabouthealth.com.
