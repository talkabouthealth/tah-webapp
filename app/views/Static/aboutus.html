#{extends 'static.html' /} #{set pageHeader:'About us' /} Welcome to the TalkAboutHealth community
TalkAboutHealth is a caring and welcoming community of people who have come together to meet, share, and learn with each other.


Our vision
Provide everyone the opportunity to get the health support, advice, and answers they need, when they need it.


 Our mission
We connect people for health support.


 Our goal
Bring together patients, family, caregivers, survivors, and professionals to create an open and compassionate community for health support.


 Our sharing philosophy
TalkAboutHealth believes in sharing our experiences and knowledge. Our purpose is to create a community to help each other and expand our collective knowledge. All conversations are archived and made available for the benefit of everyone to revisit.


What TalkAboutHealth can do for you
- Take back control after learning from survivors and experienced peers.
- Know you are not alone after connecting with others like you.
- Feel hopeful after hearing survivors' experiences.
- Be confident after getting answers from experts.
- Feel comfort after sharing with someone else.
- Feel fulfilled after helping others.


 Anonymous and secure
You will only be identified by your user name during conversations. You determine how much information to share in your profile and whether your profile is private, public, or viewable by the community. Additionally, TalkAboutHealth protects your personal information and will not share personally identifiable information such as your name and email address without your explicit permission.


Safe
TalkAboutHealth is a designated safe space. We diligently review content and monitor conversations to enforce Community Guidelines. Members are encouraged to 'flag' content they feel is not acceptable. TalkAboutHealth removes content and bans users that do not follow the Community Guidelines or that cause negative feelings for others.


 Team and advisors
TalkAboutHealth is led by a team and advisors concerned about your health. We have qualified physicians, pharmacists, and therapists on our medical advisory board.
