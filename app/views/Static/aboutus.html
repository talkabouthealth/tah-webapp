TalkAboutHealth is a breast cancer question and answer web site that is created, curated, and improved by the community. We match the right people to support each other for personalized, helpful, and accurate health information.

Everyone is encouraged to share their unique knowledge and experiences. We welcome patients, families, caregivers, survivors, and professionals to connect, share, and learn in a welcoming and caring environment. The more we share, the more lives we improve and save.


 What makes us different.
1) Every question on TalkAboutHealth should be the best resource possible, covering all points of view and perspectives. To accomplish this goal, we find and notify the right people to answer questions.

2) Trusted and accessible health information. The community curates, edits, votes for, and organizes questions and answers to make them easy to find, accessible, and understandable by everyone.

3) The right information to the right people, at the right time. We match answers and information to the people who need them, based on their disease, stage, treatments, side effects, and more. We notify members in real-time of answers to their questions and other relevant information via email, facebook, twitter, IM, and more.

4) Connect the right people to support each other. We match patients and caregivers with experienced peers, survivors, and experts who can help. Members are matched based on their disease, stage, treatments, cultural background, demographics, and more.

Our sharing philosophy
TalkAboutHealth believes in sharing our experiences and knowledge. Our purpose is to create a community to help each other and expand our collective knowledge. All conversations are archived and made available for the benefit of everyone to revisit.


What TalkAboutHealth can do for you

- Know you are not alone after connecting with others like you.
- Feel hopeful after hearing survivors' experiences.
- Be confident after getting answers from experts.
- Feel comfort after sharing with someone else.
- Feel fulfilled after helping others.


 Anonymous and secure
You will only be identified by your user name. You determine how much information to share in your profile and whether your profile is private, public, or viewable by the community. TalkAboutHealth protects your personal information and will not share personally identifiable information such as your name and email address without your explicit permission.


Safe
TalkAboutHealth is a designated safe space. We diligently review and moderate content to enforce Community Guidelines. Members are encouraged to 'flag' content they feel is not acceptable. TalkAboutHealth removes content and bans users that do not follow the Community Guidelines or that cause negative feelings for others.


 Team and advisors
TalkAboutHealth is led by a team and advisors concerned about your health. We have qualified physicians, pharmacists, and therapists on our medical advisory board.
