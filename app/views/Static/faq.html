#{extends 'static.html' /} #{set pageHeader:'FAQ' /} 1. What is TalkAboutHealth?
TalkAboutHealth is a welcoming and caring community of people who have come together to meet, share, and learn with each other.


2. How does TalkAboutHealth work?
TalkAboutHealth matches people with similar health concerns for answers and live chats.

1) Share your thoughts with followers and the rest of the community.
2) Post a question. Within minutes you will receive answers from your peers.
3) Request a live chat. Within minutes, your peers will join you for a video chat.

All answers and conversations are archived, categorized, and searchable. Members are encouraged to continually improve conversations by adding new answers, editing questions, and suggesting improvements to answers.


3. How does TalkAboutHealth match me with the right people to help?
TalkAboutHealth uses a proprietary algorithm that matches you with peers who have experience with your request and who are similar to you. Your profile info, health info, past conversations, and answers are used to measure experience and similarity. The more profile and health information you provide and the more active you are, the better we can match you for conversations.


 4. How long will it take to receive an answer or for others to join my live chat?
Our goal is to have answers and have your peers join chats within a couple of minutes. Once we receive your request, we check to see who is online and instantly notify the right members via facebook chat, IM, email, and twitter.


 5. What can I use TalkAboutHealth for?
- Meet others with similar interests and health concerns to expand your support network.
- Learn from peers and immediately benefit from the wisdom of other people who have been through similar experiences.
- Share your experiences, daily struggles, and triumphs to help others and increase the knowledge of the community.
- Talk or chat with someone who understands for instant support.


 6. What else can I do on TalkAboutHealth?
- Be notified of chats, questions, and answers relevant to you.
- Browse or search for live talks using health and medical key words.
- Search and view archives of talks, questions, and answers using health and medical key words.
- Browse or search for other members using health, medical, and personal interest keywords.
- Contribute your insights and knowledge by participating in talks, answering questions, and making improvements to current content.


7. Who can use TalkAboutHealth?
Patients, survivors, caregivers, family members, friends, physicians, pharmacists, nurses, therapists. Everyone in the health community is encouraged to share, request help, and provide support.


8. How do I get started?
Go to www.TalkAboutHealth.com and click "Sign-Up". Signing up only takes a minute.


9. What is a "Thank you"?
A "Thank you" is given by members to each other to show gratitude. If someone helps you, please give them a "Thank you." For example, "Thank you sunshine for your advice about finding a physician that is right for me. As a result, I found the perfect physician and feel so much better about asking questions."

"Thank you's" are very important to TalkAboutHealth. They help us to measure our goals of connecting people and helping each other.


10. Is TalkAboutHealth anonymous?
Yes. You will only be identified by your username during conversations. You determine how much information to share in your profile and whether your profile is viewable or private to the community.

To be absolutely sure your identity is anonymous, we recommend the following:
- Do not associate your real name with your username.
- Make your username different than other usernames you use on the internet.


 11. How do I protect my privacy?
By default, the profile and health information you provide is private. You may change your Privacy Settings to make your profile information viewable to the community.
TalkAboutHealth does not share personally identifiable information such as your name and email address without your explicit permission.


 12. Is TalkAboutHealth safe?
TalkAboutHealth is a designated safe space. We monitor and review communications and content on the site to maintain the quality of the content and enforce Community Guidelines. As well, we have a 'flagging' system and encourage Members to 'flag' users, answers, or replies that do not follow Community Guidelines or cause Members to experience negative feelings.

Users who do not follow the Community Guidelines will be banned from the Community. Unacceptable content will be removed from the site.


13. Are TalkAboutHealth conversations (live talks, questions, and answers) public?
All questions and answers on TalkAboutHealth are public and can be found by internet search engines. Only community members though may see the username and profile image associated with questions and answers. For the public, usernames associated with conversations are generic and profile images are not viewable.

Only Community Members may participate on the site, including join live talks, ask questions, and answer questions. Archives of live chats are only viewable by the community members.


14. Is it free?
Yes.


15. I forgot my password. What should I do?
To reset your password, go to Forgot Password and enter the email address you used to set up your account. An email will be sent to you with a new password.


16. How do I contact TalkAboutHealth support?
Send an e-mail to support@talkabouthealth.com. We are happy to help and always available to give assistance.


17. Why don't you have a community for my health issue?
We will open to other health communities very soon. Please let us know your request at support@talkabouthealth.com.
