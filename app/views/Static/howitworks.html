We recommend matches for you with people who can help, such as experienced peers, survivors, and experts. We match members based on experiences, disease, treatments, medications, side effects, demographics, cultural background, and much more.


Personalized information for you
We provide the support and knowledge you need based on your health and profile information.


Real-time notifications
We notify you of new answers and updates via facebook, email, instant message, and twitter so you get the latest information.


Trusted answers
We notify the right people (experienced peers, survivors, and experts) to answer your questions and provide support. We match people based on their experience and expertise.


Organized and easy to find information
We organize and curate questions and answers so that the information is trusted and easily found.


Safe environment
We diligently enforce TalkAboutHealth Community Guidelines by removing content and banning users.


 Anonymous and secure
You will only be identified by your user name during conversations. You determine how much information to share in your profile and whether your profile is private, public, or viewable by the community. Additionally, TalkAboutHealth protects your personal information and will not share personally identifiable information such as your name and email address without your explicit permission.
