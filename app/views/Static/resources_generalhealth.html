Advanced Breast Cancer Community - Comprehensive information source and online community solely dedicated to the needs of advanced (metastatic) breast cancer patients, caregivers, family and friends, and health care providers.

American Society of Clinical Oncology - Dedicated to issues unique to clinical oncology.

Brain Meets BC Org - Provides information and support for women whose breast cancer has metastasized to the brain.

Breast Cancer Watch - Providing the latest evidence-based guidance on state-of-the-art breast cancer therapies, with critical commentaries and clinical practice recommendations.

American Cancer Society - A comprehensive guide to all forms of cancer with topics including how to stay healthy, how to talk to children or family members and recent research.

Cure Today - Covers all types of cancer from diagnosis to after treatment.

Dr. Susan Love Research Foundation - Created for women and men interested in breast cancer prevention, detection, treatment, and long-term survivorship.

EmbodiWorks - Integrative cancer care.

LiveStrong - Empowering the cancer community to address the unmet needs of cancer survivors.

ManageCancer.org - Provides cancer patients and their loved ones with information and links to valuable resources. ManageCancer.org continually researches the available resources and brings the best of them together in one manageable location.

Michelle's Place - Breast cancer resource center.

National Center for Complementary and Alternative Medicine - Offers information on complimentary and alternative medicine.

Cancer Connect
This covers all types of cancers and offers side effects, info for newly diagnosed, treatments and clinical trials.

Breast Cancer Action - Launched in response to the growing concern about the number of pink ribbon products on the market. The campaign calls for more transparency and accountability by companies that take part in breast cancer fundraising, and encourages consumers to ask critical questions about pink ribbon promotions.

Triple Step for the Cure - Information on triple negative breast cancer.
