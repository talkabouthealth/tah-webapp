Contact us at volunteer@talkabouthealth.com to get further details on volunteering at TalkAboutHealth.

 Quick things you can do
1. Ask and answer questions - at least 5 to 10 questions and answers would be amazing!
2. Facebook and Twitter - "Like" TalkAboutHealth on Facebook and "follow" us on Twitter. 3. Spread the word! Tell the people you know that need health support. Tell medical professionals, health providers, treatment centers, clinics, patient advocacy organizations. We would love to collaborate
(@talkabouthealth).
4. Publicity - Mention TalkAboutHealth to bloggers and journalists you know. Send them a link to our site and recommend us. If you have a blog, blog about us!
5. Link to our site from your web site or blog.
6. Distribute TalkAboutHealth flyers and other materials at local hospitals, doctors offices, clinics, and health organizations.
7. Represent TalkAboutHealth at events by wearing TalkAboutHealth paraphernalia and handing out flyers. Paraphernalia coming soon!
8. Send us your story as a blog post or a video (support@talkabouthealth.com), and we will post it on our blog.

 Volunteer
1. Join our Event Planning committee - Help to plan and organize events and workshops. 2. Become a reviewer - Curate content on the site by editing questions, organizing questions into topics, suggesting improvements, and flagging unacceptable content. Guidelines and best practices coming soon.
3. Intern - Looking for experience? We are looking for marketing, design, and technical interns.
4. Marketing - Do you have marketing experience? Help with marketing strategy and implementation.
5. Design - Do you have design experience? Help with designing the web site and marketing materials.
6. Technology - Do you have technology experience? Help with web development, database administration, and infrastructure.
7. Start a local TalkAboutHealth support group - A set of guidelines and best practices for starting a support group is coming in the future.
8. Host a TalkAboutHealth educational or social event - A set of guidelines and best practices for hosting events is coming in the future.
9. Mentor and navigation programs coming soon!
