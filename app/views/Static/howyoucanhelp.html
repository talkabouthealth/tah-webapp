Thanks for your interest in making a difference and providing support to others. Contact us at support@talkabouthealth.com for further details on volunteering, submitting blog posts or videos, or with connections.


 Quick things you can do
1. Share your experiences and participate as much as possible by asking and answering questions and sharing thoughts.
2. Spread the word. Tell people you know who need health support or who would be great at sharing their knowledge and experiences with the community. Here is a quick form letter you can use to invite your friends: Invite Letter.
3. Like us on Facebook and follow us on Twitter.
4. Introduce us to medical professionals, health providers (treatment centers, clinics, etc.), patient advocacy organizations, and sponsors who would like to collaborate. We work with everyone.
5 . Help with PR. If you know bloggers or journalists, send them a link to the site and recommend us. If you have a blog, blog about us.
6. Link to our site from your web site or blog.
7. Send us your story via a blog post or video and we will post on our blog - support@talkabouthealth.com.


 Volunteer
1. Encourage and teach other members to use the site. 2. Curate content on the site by editing questions, organizing questions into topics, suggesting improvements, and flagging unacceptable content.
3. Distribute flyers and other materials at local hospitals, Doctors offices, clinics, and health organizations.
4. Represent TalkAboutHealth at events by wearing TalkAboutHealth paraphernalia and handing out flyers. Paraphernalia coming soon.
5. Start a local TalkAboutHealth support group. A set of guidelines and best practices for starting a support group is coming in the future.
6. Host a TalkAboutHealth event. A set of guidelines and best practices for hosting events is coming in the future.
7. Mentor and moderation programs coming soon.
