Quick things you can do

1. Ask and answer questions - at least 5 to 10 questions and answers would be amazing!
2. Facebook and Twitter - "Like" TalkAboutHealth on Facebook and "follow" us on Twitter (@talkabouthealth). Share and retweet your favorite questions and answers.
3. Spread the word! Tell the people you know that need health support. Tell medical professionals, health providers, treatment centers, clinics, patient advocacy organizations. We would love to collaborate.
4. Publicity - Mention TalkAboutHealth to bloggers and journalists you know. Send them a link to our site and recommend us. If you have a blog, blog about us!
5. Link to our site from your web site or blog.
6. Distribute TalkAboutHealth flyers and other materials at local hospitals, doctors offices, clinics, and health organizations.
7. Represent TalkAboutHealth at events by wearing TalkAboutHealth paraphernalia and handing out flyers. Paraphernalia coming soon!
8. Send us your story as a blog post or a video (support@talkabouthealth.com), and we will post it on our blog.


Volunteer Opportunities

1. Street Team - Represent TalkAboutHealth at health events. Pass out flyers, spread the word, and greet people.
2. Patient Navigation Team program coming soon! Help patients navigate their journey.
3. Event Planning Committee - Help to plan and organize educational events.
4. Workshop Planning Committee - Help to plan and organize live Q&A sessions on the web site.
5. Content Reviewer - Curate content on the site by editing questions, organizing questions into topics, suggesting improvements, and flagging unacceptable content. Guidelin5s and best practices coming soon.
6. Internship - Looking for experience? We are looking for marketing, design, and technical interns.
7. Social Media Team - Promote TalkAboutHealth via Facebook and Twitter. Encourage your friends to "Like" and "Follow" us. Share and retweet information about upcoming events and programs as well as your favorite answers and content.
8. PR (Public Relations) Team - Reach out to bloggers, web sites, radio, tv, etc. to raise awareness for TalkAboutHealth.
9. Design - Improve the desgin of the web site and marketing materials.
10. Technology - Web development, database administration, and infrastructure.
11. Start a local TalkAboutHealth support group - A set of guidelines and best practices for starting a support group is coming in the future.
12. Host a TalkAboutHealth educational or social event - A set of guidelines and best practices for hosting events is coming in the future.
