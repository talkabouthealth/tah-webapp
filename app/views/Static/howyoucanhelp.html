Contact us at support@talkabouthealth.com to get further details on volunteering at TalkAboutHealth.

 Quick things you can do
1. Ask and answer questions and share your thoughts (at least 5 to 10 questions and answers would be amazing!).
2. Spread the word! Tell the people you know that need health support or who would share their knowledge and experiences.
3. "Like" TalkAboutHealth on Facebook and "follow" us on Twitter (@talkabouthealth).
4. Mention TalkAboutHealth to medical professionals, health providers, treatment centers, clinics, patient advocacy organizations. We would love to collaborate.
5. Mention TalkAboutHealth to bloggers and journalists you know. Send them a link to our site and recommend us. If you have a blog, blog about us!
6. Link to our site from your web site or blog.
7. Send us your story as a blog post or a video (support@talkabouthealth.com), and we will post it on our blog.

 Volunteer
1. Encourage and teach other members to use this site.
2. Curate content on the site by editing questions, organizing questions into topics, suggesting improvements, and flagging unacceptable content. Guidelines and best practices coming soon.
3. Distribute TalkAboutHealth flyers and other materials at local hospitals, doctors offices, clinics, and health organizations.
4. Represent TalkAboutHealth at events by wearing TalkAboutHealth paraphernalia and handing out flyers. Paraphernalia coming soon!
5. Start a local TalkAboutHealth support group. A set of guidelines and best practices for starting a support group is coming in the future.
6. Host a TalkAboutHealth educational or social event. A set of guidelines and best practices for hosting events is coming in the future.
7. Mentor and moderation programs coming soon!
