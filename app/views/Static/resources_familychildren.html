Boarding for Breast Cancer - For teens and young adults about detection and prevention of breast cancer.

Bright Pink - For teens and young women on how to check for and learn about breast and ovarian cancer.

Breast Cancer Sisterhood - Videos on dealing with cancer and how to tell friends.

Bosom Buddies Inc. - This website offers DVD’s including one for children, one for the husbands and one about reconstructive surgery.

American Cancer Society - A comprehensive guide to all forms of cancer with topics including how to stay healthy, how to talk to children or family members and recent research.

Caring Bridge - Free patient websites connecting loved ones during serious health events.

Give Forward - A way to gather money for your loved ones medical expenses through Facebook and email.

Kidscope - Help children and families understand the effects of cancer or chemotherapy on a loved one, to provide suggestions for coping, and to develop innovative programs and materials that communicate a message of hope to diverse families coping with this crisis.

My Life Line - Free personal websites for cancer patients, caregivers and survivors.

Nowhere Hair - Offers a children’s book on talking to children about breast cancer.

Little Pink Book - A series of children’s books covering when your teacher or mom gets cancer and when mom’s breasts cancer does not go away.

Y-Me National Breast Cancer Organization - A website offering emotional support including how to ask for help, how to tell someone you have Cancer, how to talk to children, sexual dysfunction and intimacy issues.
