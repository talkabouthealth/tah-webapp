Beyond Boobs - How to organize or find a support group in your area.

Cancer Backup - The UK's Leading Cancer Information Service and Helpline.

Cancer Buddies - helps everyone find that special buddy on the internet for the purpose of sharing, caring and supporting each other through the ups and downs of their personal experiences.

Cancer Council Australia - Telephone helpline with information and support, patient support groups and programs.

Cancer Crusade - Free online publications (monthly and weekly) and other free support services for cancer survivors and caregivers.

Cancer Hope Network - Offers free, confidential one-to-one support to cancer patients undergoing cancer treatment and their families.

Cancer Support Community - Cancer support for caregivers, friends and family of cancer patients.

Cancer Survivors Unite - Provide supportive programs and informational resources to young adults, between 18 and 40 years old, affected by a cancer diagnosis.

Carers UK - Helping caregivers get the best for the person they care for, make the most of their income, stay in paid work, keep healthy, get in touch with other carers for support, find a listening ear and campaign for change.

Crossroads - Helping caregivers make a life of their own outside caring, employing over 6,000 trained professionals, providing flexible services to people of all ages and with a range of disabilities and health conditions.

Facing Cancer Together - Designed to offer support and provide information, resources and tools to help women—and those who care about them—manage the social and emotional effects of cancer.

Gilda’s Club - Support and networking groups, lectures and workshops, and social activities.

Imerman Angels - Matches cancer patients and their caregivers and family members with cancer survivors.

Mothers Supporting Daughters with Breast Cancer - Support to mothers of daughters with breast cancer.

My cancer place - a community of people with cancer, share information, give and receive support, learn from the experiences of others, and create a free web page with text and photos that you can change anytime.

SHARE - A supportive network and community of women affected by breast or ovarian cancer.

Shared Experiences - Relates stories from people who have had cancer, are newly diagnosed in their own words, anyone can upload his or her own story.

The Scott Hamilton CARES Initiative - Connect via telephone with someone who has had cancer.
